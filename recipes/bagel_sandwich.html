Bagel Sandwich

Description

This bagel sandwich is a delicious recipe I created using only a bagel, cream cheese and eggs! I always try to eat something like this every Sunday.

Ingredients

  • One bagel
  • Cream Cheese (preferably Philadelphia)
  • Two eggs

Steps

  1. Cut the bagel into two sides, and toast the bagel.
  2. While the bagel is toasting, start up the stove.
  3. After putting oil to your pan, crack two eggs and put it into the pan, and let it cook.
  4. Once the bagel is done toasting, spread cream cheese on both sides of the bagel.
  5. Flip the eggs until they seem well cooked.
  6. Carefully place the egg in between the two sides of the bagel.
  7. Voila! You're done, now enjoy!
